Our Mission

Built for critical moments

SafetySpotCM was created with a simple belief: everyone deserves a way to call for help when it matters most. Whether you're in a remote area without cell service, in an emergency situation where every second counts, or simply need peace of mind — we're here for you.

Our app works completely offline using SMS, Bluetooth, and Wi-Fi Direct to ensure your emergency alert reaches the right people. No internet required. No subscriptions. No barriers.
